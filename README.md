# Advanced Waypoints
Have you ever gotten lost from your home in your world while exploring? Now your problems are over with this Waypoint addon, which will help you create points in your world to guide you back.

![Advanced Waypoints Curseforge Logo](logo/Advanced%20Waypoints%20-%20Curseforge%20v8.png)

## How to get the Waypoint:
First you will have to create the Waypoints Item on the cartography table with 2 papers.
